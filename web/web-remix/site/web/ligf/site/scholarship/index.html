Long Island Game Farm Scholarship

Stanley and Susan Novak Scholarship Available to Long Island Seniors Pursuing Environmental Science or Veterinary Medicine

Dowload Scholarship Application Materials in PDF format.

For the second straight year The Long Island Game Farmit will award a $1,000 scholarship to a 2005 graduating Long Island high school senior who is pursuing a degree in veterinary medicine or environmental science and plans on attending a two-year or greater college or university program immediately following graduation. The application deadline is April 1, 2005.

The scholarship was established in 2004 to honor the memories of Stanley John Novak, the Game Farm's co-founder and his daughter Susan Mary Novak. In 1999, after nearly 30 years of building the Long Island Game Farm, Stanley Novak died from a heart attack at age 66. In 2001, his daughter, Susan, who worked at the Game Farm since she was five years old died from Multiple Myeloma at age 37. Both were avid animal lovers and were staunch supporters of maintaining Long Island's environment.

Students can apply by obtaining application materials through their high school guidance department. The application deadline is April 1, 2005. The winner will be announced by May 15, 2005. For more information about the Long Island Game Farm or the scholarship program, call (631) 878-6670.
