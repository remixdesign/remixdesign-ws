Visitor Information

The Long Island Game Farm is licensed and inspected by Federal, State and County agencies. All animals are under regular veterinary care.

For your safety, adults must closely supervise children at all times.

  • Do not climb or cross barrier fences.
  • Do not reach into cages
  • We allow interaction only with certain animals. Ask a staff member first.
  • For your health, and the health of our animals, please wash your hands before and after contact.
  • Please refrain from any, and all, hand to mouth activities, such as eating, smoking, etc. while interacting with animals.
