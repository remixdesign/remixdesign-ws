Big Cat Encounter

Returning for Its 3rd Year!

The Big Cat Encounter, featuring endangered and exotic lions and tigers, takes center stage at the Game Farm's 500-person amphitheater twice daily between Memorial Day and Labor Day.

Meet rescued lions and tigers up close and personal at our new show, The Big Cat Encounter. You'll laugh, be entertained and educated as you participate in this rare experience with Clayton Rosaire, the world's youngest lion and tiger trainer, and his family of endangered and exotic big cats. Plus, stop by our "Big Cat" viewing area and watch them play between our daily shows.

In between our daily shows, you'll be able to view King and Rex, who visitors will remember were lion cubs at the Game Farm only two years ago.
