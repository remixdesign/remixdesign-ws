Field Trips and Group Sales


Your main source for information
about schools on Long Island

The Long Island Game Farm's discounted group rates makes it an ideal location for your next field trip or group outing. The Long Island Game Farm is sure to be a trip that children will remember. We have loads of fun and educational activities for kids of all ages.

If you plan for a day trip, children can rest and eat their lunch in one of our large picnic areas and quickly get back to the fun! The Game Farm is the best value in your own backyard - we are conveniently located just off exit 70 on the Long Island Expressway, no more than an hour from just about anywhere on Long Island.

"Our organization gathers once a year for a company picnic. Although we have tried other places, we keep coming back to the Long Island Game Farm. You can have your typical picnic activities in a private area where we have a local restaurant cater for us. Then we take nature trails throughout the park and enjoy visiting and feeding the animals. There's a little bit to do for all ages and everyone has a great time."

- Deb Corwin, Introtek International

Pricing Information

  • $8.95 + Tax per person (Minimum of 15 or more people). Groups of 250 or more, $8.45 per person.
  • Includes rides, shows and attractions, carousel and pony rides are extra.
  • Tax exempt school groups are required to provide tax exempt form.
  • Reservations Required - one week minimum in advance.

Birthday Parties

At this time, we do not host birthday parties, but we offer a discounted group rate for your birthday guests and welcome you to set up your own party in one of our picnic areas and spend the entire day at the zoo!

Reservations are required adn the birthday child gets in free. For all other guests, our group rate of $8.95+tax applies. Children under the age of 3 are free. Minimum of 15 persons. Reservations at least one week in advance.

Please note, balloons are not allowed in our park due to the danger to the animals.

To make reservations or for more information about group sales, including field trips and birthday parties, call (631) 878-6670 and speak with Sue Miller.
