Supported by full-service Graphic & Web Design and Digital Video Production Divisions, The Public Relations and Marketing Group provides comprehensive public relations services to its clients. These services include Strategic Planning and Research, Media Relations and Publicity, Community Relations, Government Relations, Issue and Crisis Management and Political Consulting. The company's Clients includes wide array of corporate clients, including businesses, entertainment venues and restaurants. The firm also works with several Law Firms, as well as some of Long Island's leading non-profit organizations.

PRMG views public relations as principally involving the development and implementation of strategic communications plans that will best meet the client's goals by utilizing various tools, such as media relations, supported by advertising, Internet marketing and video production. Unlike most of our competitors, we guarantee our clients a minimum number of hours that we will perform on their behalf to ensure that they are being served thoroughly and consistently. Our Pricing is competitive and designed to meet the long-term needs of our clients. Our Staff is led by our President, John Zaher, who is one of the few professionals in this area who has served as a head communications director for government entities serving in both Suffolk and Nassau Counties. Please take a moment to view our service specific areas. We look forward to serving you.
