Welcome to the Public Relations and Marketing Group's web site.

After years of public relations and marketing experience in government, the private sector and legal profession, I formed PRMG to help Long Island's businesses, non-profits, government entities and law firms leverage their talents and resources by using effective communications and marketing techniques.

A full-service public relations and marketing firm such as PRMG can provide the services necessary for the business or community organization to succeed in today's society. Whether it is the crisis that faces you today, or the long-range planning that will allow your organization to expand and excel, we stand ready to provide you with the assistance you need.

From press releases, event planning and photography, presentations, newsletters, radio and television advertising, web design and maintenance, to direct marketing, PRMG can develop the tools best suited to meet the needs of your organization.

PRMG's full-service Digital Video Production Division uses the latest in digital technology to deliver broadcast quality programs, commercials and public service announcements. Our staff is at home on location or in a television studio. This level of professionalism means our clients get exactly what they want - the first time.

PRMG's executive staff has a combined 25 years experience in marketing, public relations and video production. Since 1995, its staff has won several awards for outstanding campaigns and commercials. Our clients include some of Long Island's leading organizations in their fields. Our goal is to make your business or organization as successful as it can be.

As you review our web site, please do not hesitate to call us with any additional questions you may have.

Best wishes,

John C. Zaher, Esq., President
