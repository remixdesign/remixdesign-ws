Utopia Assisted Living Services, Inc.

Utopia is pround to have been chosen as the primary care provider in 13 congregate and senior living sites throughout the state of Connecticut. Services provided at these sites may be funded by the Department of Social Services (DSS) and the Department of Econonic and Community Development (DECD), based on income/assets or paid for privately or with insurance.

Through our ALSA, Utopia provides assisted living services within congregate and senior housing facilities. We provide supportive care to help people live independently, in their own home. Clients receive the level of help and support they require, from bathing and dressing to medication reminders, homemaking and general safety. And if there's anything extra we can do, we'll do it, just like family.

Current Sites Serviced in Connecticut
Bacon Congregate, Hartford; HT Clark Assisted Living, Glastonbury, "First State Funded (New Construction) ALSA Facility"; Immanuel House, Hartford; Juniper Hill, Storrs; Komanetsky Estates, Bristol; Ludlow Commons, Norwalk; Luther Manor, Middletown; Marvin, Norwalk; Mount Carmel, Hamden; Prospect Ridge, Ridgefield; Silverbrook Estates, Orange; Tower one & Tower East, New Haven.

The Administrator, Susan Lutes, RN, has been employed by Utopia since 1998 and oversees the operation of all sites in Connecticut. She is actively involved with the Connecticut Assisted Living Association (CALA), that was established in 1995. Utopia has worked closely with the state of Connecticut in developing the Assisted Living Services program since its inception in 2001.

Utopia's Company Philosophy . . .
Utopia Assisted Living Services' health care team is committed to the delivery of appropriate, cost effective and quality service, while upholding the highest standards of client care. Clients and their families are treated with compassion, dignity and respect that surpasses the "Client Bill of Rights."

Care administered focuses on the total needs of the client. The management team at Utopia Assisted Living Services assures competent staff by providing on-going training and education.

The client and family are involved in the development of the service plan and establishment of goals. it is our belief that quality performance and service are essential in assisting clients in meeting their goals in a safe environment and maintaining independence in their home setting.

Utopia's Assisted Living Services answer a very basic human need:

  • The need we all have for independence
  • To live in familiar surroundings
  • To be treated with dignity and respect
  • To be surrounded with caring, compassionate people who treat you just like family.

We offer services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
