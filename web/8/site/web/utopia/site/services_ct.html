  • Registered Nurses

  • Live-In Aides

  • Bilingual Aides (specializing in Spanish)
  • Therapists

All our field employees are screened, trained, certified or licensed, bonded, insured and supervised by a registered nurse.

Continuing education inservice classes and workshops are provided throughout the year.

Services are offered 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and are paid for by Medicare, Medicaid, Insurance or Private Pay.

Criminal background checks are made on all personnel before they are permitted to work.

We are an approved provider of Medicare and Medicaid services throughout the state.

Utopia is accredited by the Joint Commission, a nationally recognized accrediting organization.

Most insurance plans are accepted. Free insurance evaluation and assistance with billing and forms is provided.

Our reputation for delivering highly qualified nurses, therapists, social workers and aides has enabled Utopia to contract its services with a wide variety of organizations.

The Performance Improvement Committee, composed of key administrative and clinical staff meets six times per year to review patient care complaints, outcomes and all policies and procedures. It also oversees compliance with the regulations and standards of the Department of Health and the Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations.

All of our staff are thoroughly trained to assure compliance with HIPAA notice of privacy practices.

To assure the highest ethical standards, Utopia Home Care has a Corporate Compliance Program which includes a Code of Conduct for all its employees.

All of Utopia's clients receive satisfaction evaluation forms to help us closely monitor our performance.

Utopia has been ranked three times as one of the twenty-five fastest growing private companies and has been rated the largest minority owned business on Long Island for the past nine years.

Utopia's very popular quarterly health education newsletter is published on our web site and is distributed free as a community service.

Utopia Assisted Living Services, a state liscenced assisted living service provider, provides assisted living services within congregate and senior housing facilities. It was chosen by the State of Connecticut to be the primary provider in 12 of 17 congregate sites. Services provide supportive care in bathing, dressing, medication reminders, homemaking and safety.
