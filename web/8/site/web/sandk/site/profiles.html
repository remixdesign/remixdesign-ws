Michael Sullivan, Esq.

A graduate of St. Francis College and St. John's University School of Law, Michael T. Sullivan is a former Senior Staff Attorney with the Office of Hearings and Appeals of the Social Security Administration. He has been in private practice handling Social Security Claims since 1980. He is a member of the New York State, Nassau County, and Suffolk County Bar Associations, and the Brehon Law Society.






Francis Kehoe, Esq.

A graduate of Adelphi University and Fordham University School of Law, Frank Kehoe joined Michael T. Sullivan in practice in 1995 after working with a litigation firm in Brooklyn, New York. Frank is a member of the New York State, Nassau County, and Suffolk County Bar Associations and the Brehon Law Society.
